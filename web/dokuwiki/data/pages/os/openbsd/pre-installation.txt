====== Pre-Installation ======

===== Flash USB =====

<WRAP center round important 60%>
This section is incomplete and needs further work!
</WRAP>

1. Make sure the USB filesystem is **not** mounted!!!

2. Prepare the USB:

<cli>
# cfdisk -z <DEVICE>
cfdisk> gpt
cfdisk> n
cfdisk>  (Enter)
cfdisk> W
cfdisk> yes
cfdisk> Q
# wipefs -a 
</cli>

3. Flash disk image on the USB:

<cli>
# dd if=~/openbsd/install<VERSION>.iso of=<DEVICE> bs=1M
</cli>

===== USB Boot =====

1. Reboot the system and during the POST phase press //BIOS key//((List of //POST keys// according to vendor: http://www.disk-image.com/faq-bootmenu.htm.)) to boot into UEFI.

2. Disable //Secure Boot// and reboot.

3. On the next POST phase, press //Boot Menu key//((List of //POST keys// according to vendor: http://www.disk-image.com/faq-bootmenu.htm.)) and select USB entry.
