===== Pros =====

  *Security((Security policy: https://www.openbsd.org/security.html.)).
  *Wide processor architecture support((Supported platforms: https://www.openbsd.org/plat.html.)).
  *UNIX-like OS((Unix-like operating systems: https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Unix-like.)).
  *Documentation((Handbook: https://www.openbsd.org/faq/index.html.))((Manpages: https://man.openbsd.org.)).
  *Free software only((Binary blobs: https://www.openbsd.org/lyrics.html#39.)).

===== Cons =====

  *Point release model.
  *Small community & amount of software supported.
  *Supported software((Packages: https://openports.se/statistics.php.)).
